This text is about Göncöl táltos(mage/shaman)

Döncöl Gáltos was a person in Hungarian mythology, beliefs, similar to Gandalf from Lord of the rings.

A constellation is named after him "Göncölszekér" (carriage of Göncöl) which is part of the Ursa Maiorc onstellation.
